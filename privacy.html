SYNTHEXIA

Global Privacy Policy & Data Architecture

Last Updated: June 2026

1 Introduction & Core Philosophy

Welcome to the Synthexia Ecosystem. We are committed to maintaining the highest standard of data privacy and systemic integrity. As a highly advanced RPG and Economy application for Discord, we prioritize seamless functionality without compromising user security.

This policy outlines our strict, transparent data handling protocols. By utilizing the Synthexia bot and interacting with our commands, you consent to the operations detailed within this document.

2 Information Collection Protocols

To sustain the complex infrastructure of real estate tracking, faction hierarchies, and advanced global economies, Synthexia strictly collects only the necessary architectural data provided by the Discord API. We do not use external APIs or invasive tracking methods.

  • Cryptographic IDs: We collect your Discord User ID and the Server (Guild) ID to establish database profiles for your wealth, properties, and clans.
  • Transactional Ledgers: For transparent financial history, the system explicitly records only your last 10 transactions. Older data is permanently overwritten to ensure data minimization.
  • Command Execution Data: We process message context purely when triggering commands (e.g., `!bal`, `!pay`) to ensure flawless execution. We do not read or store regular server conversations.

3 Data Processing & Utilization

All gathered data operates exclusively within our native engine to deliver the massive RPG experience. The collected information is strictly used for:

  • Managing dynamic Currency variables and applying global taxation (TaxRate).
  • Tracking ownership of digital assets, including residential complexes, commercial empires, and royal castles.
  • Ensuring the flawless execution of server-wide administrative events like simulated market crashes and loot drops.

4 Unrivaled Security Architecture

Synthexia's backend is heavily fortified. We reject standard bot vulnerabilities through our custom-built logic:

  • Zero External APIs: The entire database is 100% self-contained natively. Data is never passed through third-party webhooks or analytical tools.
  • BankLocked Systems: Wealth and assets are secured via proprietary BankLocked logic, preventing unauthorized financial drains or exploits.
  • Standardized Backend: To ensure zero corruption and maximum execution speed, all internal variables and server logs operate on strict English nomenclature, while providing clean, multi-language localized outputs for the user.

5 Third-Party Disclosure

We absolutely do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your Personally Identifiable Information (PII) or digital asset data to outside parties. Synthexia is an independent empire, and your data remains sealed within our infrastructure. This strict protocol ensures that your digital wealth, faction strategies, and real estate portfolios cannot be monitored by external entities.

6 User Rights & Data Erasure

You maintain absolute control over your digital footprint within the Synthexia Metaverse.

If you wish to liquidate your assets, dissolve your factions, and completely erase your profile from the Synthex Central Bank (SCB), you hold the right to request full data deletion. Server administrators may also request the expulsion of all Guild data from our primary nodes.

For erasure requests, please contact the lead developer via our official Discord Support channels or through the direct command prompts provided in-app.
