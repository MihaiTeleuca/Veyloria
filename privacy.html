Privacy policy

Transparent data rules for Veyloria.

This policy explains what information Veyloria may process, why it is needed, how it may be used and what users can do if they have privacy questions.

Veyloria should collect only what is needed for bot operation, support, economy features, safety and future premium access once official payment systems are released.

Privacy note.

This Privacy Policy is a detailed public template for Veyloria. It is written to be transparent and data-minimization friendly, but it should be reviewed before official launch, especially if Veyloria begins processing payments, running automated premium access or storing more account data.

Overview

What this policy covers.

This Privacy Policy explains how Veyloria may collect, use, store and protect information when users interact with the Veyloria website, Discord bot, support server, command directory, documentation, premium information and related official services.

Veyloria is designed to collect only the information needed to operate the bot, provide support, maintain economy features, prevent abuse and improve the service. The project should avoid collecting sensitive information and should never ask users to provide Discord tokens, passwords, recovery codes, private keys or payment card details inside Discord support tickets.

  • This policy applies to Veyloria-controlled services and official Veyloria support spaces.
  • Discord and other third-party services have their own privacy practices.
  • Premium payments are not active yet; payment processing details should be finalized before launch.
  • Users should use the official support server for privacy questions or data requests.

Data collected

Information Veyloria may process.

When Veyloria is used in a Discord server, the bot may process basic Discord identifiers and command-related data. This is usually necessary for features such as profiles, economy balances, cooldowns, logs, leaderboards, support reports, moderation review and premium access checks once premium becomes active.

Not collected

Information Veyloria should not request.

Veyloria support does not need private account secrets to help users. If anyone asks for secret credentials while claiming to represent Veyloria, users should treat it as suspicious and report it through the official support server.

  • Discord user tokens or bot tokens.
  • Passwords, recovery codes or two-factor authentication codes.
  • Private keys, hosting secrets or environment variables.
  • Payment card numbers inside Discord tickets or private messages.
  • Personal documents unless a future official legal process specifically requires them, which is not part of normal support.

Use of data

Why information is used.

Veyloria uses information to operate bot features, remember configuration, provide economy systems, prevent abuse, answer support requests and maintain safer community spaces. The bot should avoid using information for unrelated purposes that users would not reasonably expect.

  • Bot operation: responding to commands, storing balances, managing inventory and showing profiles.
  • Server configuration: remembering channels, enabled modules, currency settings and permission-related choices.
  • Support: investigating bug reports, setup issues, suspicious activity and user questions.
  • Security: detecting abuse, scams, exploit attempts, duplicate reports or suspicious economy behavior.
  • Premium: once active, verifying premium access and applying the correct server or user benefits.

Legal basis

Data protection approach.

Where privacy laws such as the GDPR apply, Veyloria should aim to process personal data under appropriate legal bases such as providing the requested bot service, legitimate interest in security and abuse prevention, user consent where required, or compliance with legal obligations where applicable.

Veyloria should follow practical privacy principles: be transparent, collect only what is needed, use data for defined purposes, keep data accurate where possible, limit storage duration, protect the data and provide a reasonable way for users to ask privacy questions.

  • Service operation: needed to provide bot features users request.
  • Legitimate interest: security, abuse prevention, logs and support review.
  • Consent: optional features, optional public profile elements or future marketing if ever used.
  • Legal obligation: if a valid legal request requires specific action.

Sharing

When information may be shared.

Veyloria should not sell user data. Information may be shared only when needed to operate the service, comply with platform rules, use hosting or payment providers, respond to support cases or handle safety and legal issues.

  • Discord: Veyloria operates inside Discord, so bot actions and server context naturally involve Discord's platform.
  • Hosting providers: website and backend infrastructure may process technical data needed to host the service.
  • Payment processors: once premium payments are active, payment providers may process checkout and billing information under their own policies.
  • Staff: authorized staff may review support reports, logs and configuration context when needed.
  • Legal or safety reasons: information may be disclosed if required to protect users, comply with valid legal obligations or prevent abuse.

Retention

How long data may be kept.

Retention periods may vary depending on the type of data. Economy state may be stored while the bot is active in a server. Logs may be kept for support, security and audit review. Support tickets may be retained long enough to resolve the case and review repeated issues. Data that is no longer needed should be deleted or anonymized where practical.

Security

How data should be protected.

Veyloria should use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect data from unauthorized access, misuse, loss or alteration. Security is a shared responsibility: users must also protect their Discord accounts, server permissions, bot tokens and private credentials.

  • Keep bot tokens, API keys and payment secrets outside public website files.
  • Limit staff access to logs and support data to people who need it.
  • Use role hierarchy and permission checks to reduce accidental exposure.
  • Review suspicious activity, impersonation attempts and fake premium links.
  • Encourage users to report unsafe messages through official support paths.

User rights

Privacy requests.

Depending on location and applicable law, users may have rights to ask about their personal data, request correction, request deletion, object to certain processing, request restriction or ask for a copy of certain information. Veyloria should provide a reasonable support path for privacy requests.

Some data may need to be kept for security, abuse prevention, support history, legal reasons or technical integrity. If a request cannot be fully completed, the user should receive an explanation through the official support path.

Minors

Age and server responsibility.

Veyloria operates on Discord and depends on Discord account access and server settings. Users must follow Discord's age requirements and the rules of the servers where Veyloria is used. Server owners are responsible for deciding whether their community is appropriate for their audience and how economy features are presented to members.

Veyloria should not knowingly request sensitive personal information from children or require personal documents for normal bot use.

Changes

Updates to this policy.

This Privacy Policy may be updated as Veyloria moves toward official release, activates premium payments, adds backend automation or changes how data is processed. Material updates should be announced on the website or official support server.
