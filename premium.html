Premium access

Premium built around support, clarity and growth.

Veyloria Premium is planned as an optional upgrade for users and server owners who want a more refined support experience, better guidance, early product information and extra recognition inside the Veyloria ecosystem.

Premium is not required to understand the bot or use the core experience. The main purpose is to support development, improve access to advanced guidance and give active communities a cleaner path when they need help with setup, economy planning or future premium systems.

Payments are not available yet.

Veyloria Premium plans are displayed for transparency and preparation, but payments cannot be processed yet. Premium purchases will become available only after the official public version of the bot is published. Until then, all premium buttons will redirect to the official Discord support server for information and updates.
